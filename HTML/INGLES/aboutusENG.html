About us


Canrisk is an informative website with the purpose of informing the society in an accessible and reliable way about the most common types of cancer in the Salvadoran population: breast, cervical, prostate, stomach and lung cancer.

Our project is called CANRISK because it comes from the combination of two important words, which are cancer (Cáncer) and risk, giving the union Can-Risk. The fusion of both words was done to give it a more unique and attractive approach for our website users.
